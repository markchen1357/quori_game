Familiarizing Yourself with the Game

Let's go over how this study will work. Click the arrow on the right to go to the next screen.

You will be playing a sorting game where you place each card in either the left bin or the right bin.

The cards have four different properties: color, number, shape, and shading. These properties determine which bin the card belongs in based on a rule.

Meet the robot who will help you through the game.

We will be recording your facial features as you play the game. You have already checked that your camera is working.

Let's go over what you will be doing. You will be playing the game through 2 times, with a different rule each time.

The robot will choose a rule and will play a few rounds of the game.

Then it will be your turn to play! Make your best guess about where the card goes. Drag it to either the left or right bin.

You will choose how confident you are in your choice.

The robot will give you feedback about whether you got it right. And then you will sort another card.

After you finish sorting all the cards, you will complete a short survey about your experience.

After the survey, the robot will give you a new rule to learn.

After playing the game twice, you will receive a random code to enter into Prolific so you receive credit for completing this survey.
